文艺复欣
一位爱瞎想的手作人。平时寡言文静，偶尔话痨沙雕。内心永远充满色彩斑斓的灵感。
主打售卖各色自制手帐本，火漆挂件，手机链等。每一件手作物都在等待一位适合它的有缘人。现也在探索将AI与手帐结合。
希望这些手作小物，能让你也感受到记录生活的乐趣，遇见那个多彩而独特的自己。
A daydreaming handicrafts person. Usually quiet and reserved, but sometimes talkative and silly. The heart is always filled with colorful inspirations.
Mainly selling various self-made handcrafts such as hand-made notebooks, wax seal pendants, and mobile phone chains. Each handicraft is waiting for its destined owner. I'm also exploring ways to integrate AI with notebooks.
Hope these small handcrafts can also make you feel the joy of recording life and meet that colorful and unique self.
